# Infrastructure Drives Real Estate

In the **Pune Real Estate Market**, land values are inextricably linked to macro-infrastructure developments. While commercial real estate (office space) relies on talent pools, residential land values rely purely on connectivity. 

Currently, the Hinjewadi-Marunji corridor is undergoing the most aggressive infrastructure overhaul in the history of Maharashtra, making **NA Bungalow Plots** in this region the most heavily sought-after asset.

## 1. Pune Metro Line 3 (Hinjewadi to Shivajinagar)

The 23-kilometer elevated Metro Line 3 is completely reshaping transit. What historically took 90 minutes in bumper-to-bumper traffic will soon take 35 minutes in air-conditioned comfort. 

For residents of **Kumar Aranya Bungalow Plots in Hinjewadi**, this means:
* You have the serenity of a lakeside forest sanctuary.
* You are insulated from the city's noise and pollution.
* Yet, you are less than 40 minutes away from the absolute center of Pune city via the Metro.

Properties within a 5km radius of a metro station historically appreciate by a massive 30-45% within the first 3 years of the station becoming operational.

## 2. The PMRDA Outer Ring Road

The proposed 128 km PMRDA Ring Road is designed to divert heavy traffic away from the city center, connecting major highways (Mumbai-Pune Expressway, Solapur Highway, Nashik Highway). A major node of this Ring Road passes extremely close to the Marunji/Hinjewadi Phase 1 intersection.

This dramatically reduces commute times to Mumbai and the proposed New Pune International Airport in Purandar. 

## The Marunji Advantage: Kumar Aranya

Hinjewadi Phase 1, 2, and 3 are largely saturated with commercial IT parks and high-density apartments. Marunji, sitting adjacent to Phase 1, is the last remaining bastion of natural topography (lakes and hills). 

**Kumar Aranya** has capitalized on this by acquiring the most premium parcels of land in Marunji. By securing an **NA Bungalow Plot** here, you are positioning yourself at the exact geographical epicenter of Pune's multi-billion dollar infrastructure boom, while maintaining a lifestyle of uncompromising luxury.
