The Fall of Luxury Apartments

For the past decade, HNWIs poured capital into 4BHK and 5BHK luxury apartments. The mathematical reality of 2026 is stark: apartments are depreciating concrete structures sitting on a negligible, undivided share of land. Maintenance costs erode rental yields, resulting in stagnant net wealth.

The Rise of NA Bungalow Plots

Conversely, NA Bungalow Plots in Pune offer 100% land ownership. Because land supply is finite (especially within PMRDA limits near major IT hubs), basic supply-and-demand economics guarantee appreciation. Gated plot communities offer the exact same lifestyle amenities as luxury high-rises (clubhouses, security, landscaping) but with zero depreciation and absolute sovereignty.
